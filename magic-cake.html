Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of suger
  • 3 spoons of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs , suger and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a nonstick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Lets sit at room temperature for 20 min
  5. Eat and enjoy
